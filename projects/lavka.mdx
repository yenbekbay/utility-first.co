_Lavka.io_ was an Almaty-based platform for on-demand delivery of goods from any restaurant or store in cities across Kazakhstan.

As founding partners, we:

- Conducted product strategy and discovery.
- Designed a unique brand experience, flexible information architecture and interfaces for a variety of devices and platforms.
- Implemented the backend, the iOS and Android mobile apps for clients, the web app for couriers, the admin dashboard, and other internal tools.

Lavka.io had a small operation in Kazakhstan from 2017 to 2019 before having to shut down under market pressure.
